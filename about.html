About Us

Where it Began

It all started with Payton, a kid who couldn’t get enough of sneakers—collecting them, learning their stories, and showing them off at school. What began as a personal passion quickly turned into a hustle, as Payton started reselling shoes to friends and fellow sneakerheads. That spark grew into campgn, a trusted reselling platform built to connect others who share the same love for kicks. Founded by true enthusiasts, we’ve created a space where finding authentic, high-demand sneakers is as exciting and reliable as owning them. Every pair tells a story—and we're here to help you find yours.

What We Do

Here at campgn, we specialize in sourcing and reselling the most exclusive sneakers on the market. From limited-edition drops to timeless classics, our inventory is curated for collectors, trendsetters, and everyday wearers alike. Whether you're searching for a grail you've been chasing or just looking to upgrade your rotation, we make the process simple, secure, and transparent.

Our Promise

Authenticity is everything. Every pair that passes through our hands is verified by trained experts to ensure that what you’re getting is 100% legit. We take pride in our rigorous authentication process so our buyers and sellers can trade with confidence. No fakes, no surprises—just real heat.

What Makes Us Different?

We’re not just another resell site—we’re a community. Built by sneaker lovers for sneaker lovers, we’re here to offer more than just shoes. We offer insights, stories, and a culture that celebrates the creativity, history, and energy behind every pair. Our customer-first approach, fast shipping, and dedicated support set us apart in a crowded space.

Join the Movement

Whether you’re buying your first pair or your fiftieth, campgn is here to make your sneaker journey unforgettable. Stay ahead of the drop, discover hidden gems, and become part of a growing network of passionate resellers and buyers.
