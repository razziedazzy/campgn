Urban Runner

Lightweight, breathable, and designed for everyday movement. The Urban Runner combines modern style with all-day comfort for city life.

  • - Flyknit upper for adaptive support
  • - React foam midsole for responsive cushioning
  • - Rubber outsole with multidirectional traction
  • - Reflective details for low-light visibility

Technology

Flyknit

Engineered yarn wraps provide targeted support and a snug, sock-like fit.

React Foam

Lightweight and durable, delivering a smooth, responsive ride.

Rubber Outsole

High-abrasion rubber in key areas for traction that lasts.

Reflective Elements

Strategic detailing ensures visibility in low-light conditions.
